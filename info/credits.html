Spotify Bot About:

Spotify Bot was created with the intention of giving information of music, artists, albums, playlists with the spotify api but also attached commands to play music from youtube and this is how the project began to develop.

Note:

The bot won't and can't play music from Spotify, as that is illegal and against Spotify's Terms of Service. Instead, this bot will only play music from YouTube. Thank you for understanding.
